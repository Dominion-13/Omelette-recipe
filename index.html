Easy Omelette Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 Tablespoon butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: cheese, vegetables, herbs

Instructions:

1. Crack the Eggs into a bowl, add a pinch of salt and pepper and whisk until it is well mixed.

2. Heat the butter in a non-stick pan over medium heat.

3. Pour the eggs into the pan, tilting the pan to spread them evenly.

4. Cook for 1-2 minutes until the edges start to set.

5. Add any optional fillings like chesse or vegetables on one half of the Omelette

6. Gentely fold the other half over the fillings.

7. Cook for another one to two minutes[1-2] until the eggs are fully set.

8. Slide the Omelette onto a plate and serve hot.

Enjoy your Omlette
